About Me

Born in Manhattan Beach, California, and raised in Fort Collins, Colorado, Zach has been passionate about both physical and mental fitness from a young age. He earned his degree in Health and Exercise Science with a focus in Health Promotion from Colorado State University in 2022. When he's not in the training or working out in the gym, Zach can be found hiking, skiing, surfing, and exploring new destinations. Zach thrives on the opportunity to collaborate with athletes across all age groups and fitness levels to increase their overall confidence by pushing them to reach their mental and physical goals. By closely collaborating with each client, he designs customized programs that not only drive progress, but also foster lasting, lifestyle changes. With a well-rounded background in sports performance, track and field, skiing and snowboarding, bodybuilding, powerlifting, and injury rehab, he brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to every training session. Together, you'll push past limits, break through barriers, and unlock your true potential to become the best version of yourself.
